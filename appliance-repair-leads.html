3 Proven Ways to Generate Appliance Repair Leads

Google Ads for Appliance Repair

Target high-intent keywords like "refrigerator repair near me" and "broken dishwasher help." Google Ads puts your business in front of customers exactly when their appliances fail.

  • Target appliance-specific repair searches
  • High-conversion emergency lead focus
  • Geo-fencing for your local service area
  • Call-only ads for mobile searchers
Average Cost Per Lead: $30-$70

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Build brand awareness so you're the first repair company homeowners think of. Target local homeowners with tips on appliance maintenance and special offer coupons.

  • Target local homeowners by zip code
  • Promote maintenance service specials
  • Retarget people who visited your site
  • Build trust with video testimonials
Average Cost Per Lead: $25-$60

SEO for Appliance Repair

Dominate local search results for "brand + repair" keywords. Build long-term authority and organic traffic that generates leads without a per-click cost.

  • Rank for "brand name repair" keywords
  • Optimize Google Business Profile for maps
  • Create content for appliance care tips
  • Acquire local backlinks and citations
Timeline to Results: 3-6 months
