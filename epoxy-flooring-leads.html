3 Proven Ways to Generate Epoxy Flooring Leads

Google Ads for Epoxy Flooring

Capture homeowners actively searching for "epoxy garage floor" and floor coating services. Google Ads puts you at the top of search results when people are ready to invest in flooring.

  • Target high-value garage floor searches
  • Geo-target your service area
  • Showcase project portfolios in ads
  • Track quote requests and consultations
Average Cost Per Lead: $40-$120

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Reach homeowners with stunning before/after transformations of garage floors. Visual ads showcasing your work inspire homeowners to start their own flooring projects.

  • Showcase dramatic floor transformations
  • Target homeowners planning renovations
  • Highlight color and finish options
  • Retarget website visitors
Average Cost Per Lead: $50-$140

SEO for Epoxy Flooring

Rank organically for local epoxy flooring searches. Build long-term visibility without ongoing ad spend through optimized content and local SEO strategies.

  • Rank for "epoxy garage floor" searches
  • Optimize Google Business Profile
  • Create project galleries and guides
  • Build portfolio-based content
Timeline to Results: 3-6 months
