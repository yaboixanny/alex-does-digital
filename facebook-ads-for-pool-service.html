Facebook Ads FAQs

Do you use Facebook Lead Forms or a Landing Page?

We test both, but for pool service, native Lead Forms often win. They keep the user in the app and allow us to ask qualifying questions like "Do you have a pool?" and "What's your biggest pain point?"

What kind of "Offer" works best?

A "free salt-water transformation" or "First 2 weeks of maintenance free with a 6-month contract" are huge drivers. We focus on getting you in the door to build the relationship.

How many leads should I expect?

A typical $1,000/month budget usually generates between 30-50 high-intent homeowner leads in most markets.
