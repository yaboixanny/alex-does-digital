3 Proven Ways to Generate Garage Door Leads

Google Ads for Garage Door Companies

Capture homeowners searching for "garage door repair near me" and installation services. Google Ads puts your company at the top when garage doors break and people need immediate help.

  • Target emergency repair searches
  • Promote installation and replacement
  • Call-only ads for mobile searches
  • Track calls and job bookings
Average Cost Per Lead: $25-$55

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Build demand with before/after installation photos and showcase your work quality. Target homeowners in your service area to generate installation leads and brand awareness.

  • Showcase installation transformations
  • Target homeowners by demographics
  • Promote seasonal offers and financing
  • Build local brand recognition
Average Cost Per Lead: $30-$65

SEO for Garage Door Companies

Rank organically for local garage door searches. Dominate map pack results and generate free leads for repairs and installations without ongoing ad spend.

  • Rank for "garage door repair near me"
  • Dominate Google Maps listings
  • Create service-specific pages
  • Build reviews and local authority
Timeline to Results: 3-6 months
