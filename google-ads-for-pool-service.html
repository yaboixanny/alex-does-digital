High-Intent Lead Generation

Google Ads is the most powerful tool for pool service companies because it allows you to intercept "Demand" the moment it happens. But success requires more than just picking keywords.

1. Segmenting Intent

We split our campaigns into two main categories:

  • Emergency/Repair: Targeting terms like "pool pump repair," "green pool help," or "leak detection." These have high CPCs but convert into immediate, high-ticket jobs.
  • Maintenance/Contracts: Targeting "pool cleaning service," "weekly pool maintenance," or "pool companies near me." These are the lifeblood of your recurring revenue.

2. Negative Keyword Mastery

Most pool companies burn 40% of their budget on "DIY" searches. We aggressively block terms like:

  • "How to clean pool filter"
  • "Cheap pool supplies"
  • "Jobs," "Salary," "Training"
  • Commercial terms (if you only do residential)

Our 4-Step Launch Process

01

Account Structure

We build granular "Single Theme" ad groups. If someone searches for "Salt Water Pool Service," they see an ad specifically about salt water expertise, not a generic cleaning ad.

02

Ad Copy Alpha

We use dynamic keyword insertion and highlight scarcity: "Only 3 Weekly Spots Left in [City Name]." This creates urgency that drives the click.

03

LP Optimization

We don't send traffic to your homepage. We send it to a dedicated pool service landing page with a one-click "Call Now" button and verified local reviews.

04

Daily Bid Management

We monitor search terms daily, adjusting bids based on which neighborhoods are converting best and which keywords are driving the highest quality "Contract" leads.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long until I see leads?

Google Ads is instant. Most clients start receiving phone calls within 24-48 hours of the campaign going live.

What is the minimum monthly budget?

For pool service, we recommend at least $1,000/month to be competitive in most markets and gather enough data to optimize effectively.

Do you track actual phone calls?

Yes. We use dynamic number insertion (DNI) to track precisely which keyword drove the call, and we can even record them for your lead quality review.
