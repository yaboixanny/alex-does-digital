Towing Company Marketing

Why Google Ads Works for Towing Companies

When someone's car breaks down, they're searching for help immediately. Google Ads for towing companies puts your phone number directly in front of these high-intent customers at the exact moment they need your service.

Frequently Asked Questions: Google Ads for Towing Companies

How much does Google Ads cost for towing companies?

The average cost per call for towing Google Ads is $15-30, with a 30-40% conversion rate to paying jobs. Most towing companies spend $500-2,000/month on Google Ads depending on their service area size.

What keywords should I target for towing Google Ads?

Focus on high-intent emergency keywords like:

  • "tow truck near me"
  • "emergency towing"
  • "24 hour tow truck"
  • "roadside assistance"
  • "car towing service [your city]"

Add your specific city and surrounding areas to capture local searches.

Are call-only ads better for towing companies?

Yes, call-only ads are ideal for towing. They display a clickable call button on mobile devices that immediately initiates a phone call - perfect for emergency situations when customers need help immediately. These ads only show on mobile devices where the call functionality works.

How do I target my service area in Google Ads?

Use radius targeting around your business location (typically 10-25 miles) or set up location campaigns for specific cities. Set up location extensions to display your business address and service radius. This prevents wasted spend on calls outside your service area.

How long does it take to see results from Google Ads?

Most towing companies see results within 1-2 weeks. However, it takes 2-3 months of continuous optimization to lower cost per call and maximize ROI. Google Ads requires ongoing management - the key is continuous A/B testing and bid adjustments.

Can I run Google Ads 24/7 for towing?

Yes, and you should. Car emergencies happen at all hours. We recommend running campaigns 24/7 but adjusting bids based on time of day. Increase bids during peak emergency hours (typically 6-9 AM and 5-9 PM) and lower them during slow periods.

What's a good cost per lead for towing companies?

For towing, the benchmark is $15-30 per call. With a 30-40% conversion rate to jobs, each call is worth $100-150 in revenue. If you're paying more than $35 per call, it's time to optimize your campaign.

Should I use Google Performance Max for towing?

We recommend starting with Search campaigns for towing - they're more predictable and easier to optimize. Performance Max can work but requires more data and testing. Start with Search, then add Performance Max once you have conversion data.

How do I track Google Ads conversions for towing?

Use Google Call Tracking or services like CallRail to track which keywords and ads generate calls. Set up conversion actions for phone calls in Google Ads. Monitor which keywords, ad copy, and times of day drive your best leads.

What if I only want to target my immediate city?

Use location targeting to specify exact cities or zip codes. Set a radius around your business (we recommend 15-20 miles for towing). Use negative keywords to exclude areas you don't serve.
