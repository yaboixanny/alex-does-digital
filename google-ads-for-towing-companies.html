How to Set Up Google Ads for Your Towing Company

Setting up effective Google Ads for towing companies requires understanding your local market and emergency call patterns. Here's what makes a successful campaign:

1

Target High-Intent Keywords

Focus on emergency and location-based keywords like "tow truck near me," "emergency towing," "24 hour tow truck," and "roadside assistance." These searches indicate immediate need and convert at higher rates.

2

Use Call-Only Campaigns

Call-only ads are specifically designed for mobile devices and display your phone number prominently. When someone clicks your ad, it immediately initiates a phone call - perfect for emergency towing situations.

3

Set Up Geo-Targeting

Only show ads in your actual service area. This prevents wasted spend on calls you can't fulfill and ensures you're capturing customers you can actually help.

4

Run Ads 24/7

Car emergencies happen at all hours. Make sure your Google Ads campaigns run around the clock to capture emergency calls whenever they happen.

5

Optimize Continuously

Once your campaign is live, continuous optimization is key to lowering your cost per call and increasing conversion rates. Use call tracking to understand which ads generate the highest quality leads.
