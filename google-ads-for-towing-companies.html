Common Questions About Google Ads for Towing

How much does Google Ads cost for towing?

The average cost per call is $15-30, with a 30-40% conversion rate to paying jobs. Most towing companies spend $500-2,000/month depending on market competition and service area size.

What keywords should I target?

Focus on high-intent emergency keywords:

  • "tow truck near me"
  • "emergency towing [city]"
  • "24 hour towing"
  • "roadside assistance"
  • "flatbed tow truck"

Are call-only ads better for towing?

Yes. Call-only ads show a prominent call button on mobile devices and drive direct phone calls. They're perfect for towing because customers need immediate help — no time to fill out forms.

How long until I see results?

Most towing companies see results within 1-2 weeks. However, it takes 2-3 months of optimization to lower cost per call and maximize profitability.

Should I run ads 24/7?

Yes. Car emergencies happen at all hours. We recommend running 24/7 but adjusting bids higher during peak hours (6-9 AM, 5-9 PM) and lower during slow periods.

Can I limit ads to my service area?

Absolutely. Use radius targeting (10-25 miles around your location) or location campaigns for specific cities. Add location extensions to display your address. This prevents wasted spend on calls you can't service.
