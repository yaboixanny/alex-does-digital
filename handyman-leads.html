Why Handymen Need Digital Marketing

When homeowners need repairs, installations, or home improvements, they search online for reliable handyman services. From fixing doors to mounting TVs to deck repairs, people search locally for help. If you're not showing up when they're searching, they're calling your competitors. 3 Proven Ways to Generate Handyman Leads

Google Ads for Handymen

Capture homeowners actively searching for "handyman near me" and specific repair services. Google Ads puts you at the top of search results when people need help with home repairs.

  • Target specific repair and installation searches
  • Geo-target your service area
  • Highlight same-day availability
  • Track phone calls and quote requests
Average Cost Per Lead: $20-$60

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Reach homeowners with visual before/after photos of your work. Build brand awareness so you're the first handyman they think of when they need home repairs or improvements.

  • Showcase before/after project photos
  • Target homeowners in your service area
  • Retarget website visitors
  • Build trust with customer testimonials
Average Cost Per Lead: $25-$70

SEO for Handymen

Rank organically for local handyman searches. Build long-term visibility without ongoing ad spend through optimized content and local SEO strategies.

  • Rank for "handyman near me" searches
  • Optimize Google Business Profile
  • Create service-specific content pages
  • Build local reviews and citations
Timeline to Results: 3-6 months
