Handyman Lead Generation FAQs

How much do handyman leads cost?

Handyman lead costs vary by location and lead quality. Google Ads typically range from $20–$60 per lead, while Facebook Ads average $25–$70 per lead. The actual cost depends on your market competitiveness and how well your ads are optimized.

Which platform is best for handyman leads — Google Ads or Facebook Ads?

Google Ads works better for high-intent searches (people actively searching "handyman near me"), while Facebook Ads is great for building awareness and showcasing your work. Most handymen use both for consistent lead flow. Google Ads generates faster results, while Facebook Ads can be more cost-effective at scale.

How long does it take to get handyman leads?

Google Ads can start generating leads within 24–48 hours of launching. Facebook Ads typically need 3–7 days to optimize. SEO takes 3–6 months to show results but provides long-term, ongoing lead flow without ad spend.

What's the best budget to start with for handyman lead generation?

Start with at least $15–$20/day ($450–$600/month) for meaningful volume. This gives the platform enough data to optimize properly. Most successful handymen scale to $30–$100/day once they've validated what works in their market.

Can I get handyman leads without paid ads?

Yes. SEO (optimizing your website and Google Business Profile) can generate organic handyman leads, but it takes 3–6 months to see traction. Referrals also work but are unpredictable. Most successful handymen combine Google Ads (for fast leads) with SEO (for long-term growth).

How do I qualify handyman leads?

Use your first call to qualify: Is the job within your service area? Is the budget reasonable? Can you schedule the work soon? Fast follow-up (within 5–10 minutes) helps you qualify before competitors call. Ask clear qualifying questions to avoid wasting time on low-value leads.
