3 Proven Ways to Generate HVAC Leads

Google Ads for HVAC Companies

Capture emergency repair searches and seasonal installation demand. Google Ads delivers your phone number to homeowners when their HVAC system breaks or needs replacement.

  • Target emergency and installation searches
  • Seasonal campaign optimization
  • Call-only ads for urgent requests
  • Promote financing and maintenance plans
Average Cost Per Lead: $45-$95

Facebook & Instagram Ads

Build brand awareness before emergencies happen. Target homeowners with aging HVAC systems and promote seasonal tune-ups, maintenance contracts, and system upgrades.

  • Promote seasonal maintenance contracts
  • Target homeowners by property age
  • Showcase installation transformations
  • Build trust before emergency needs
Average Cost Per Lead: $50-$110

SEO for HVAC Companies

Rank organically for "HVAC repair near me" and installation searches. Dominate local map pack and capture high-value emergency calls without ongoing ad spend.

  • Rank for emergency repair searches
  • Dominate Google Maps for local HVAC
  • Create service-specific landing pages
  • Build authority through reviews
Timeline to Results: 4-6 months
